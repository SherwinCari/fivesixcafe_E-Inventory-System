ABOUT US

At Fivesix Legaspi Cafe, we believe that great coffee starts with great beans. Our mission is to provide coffee enthusiasts with the finest selection of beans sourced from around the world. Whether you're a seasoned barista or just starting your coffee journey, we have something for everyone. Join us in celebrating the art of coffee-making and experience the rich flavors that only Fivesix Legaspi Cafe can offer.

OUR STORY

Born from a passion for exceptional coffee and community, FIVESIX Legaspi Cafe opened its doors in 2021. What started as a simple dream between friends has grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering spot where every cup tells a story.

We meticulously source our beans and craft each drink with care, blending traditional brewing techniques with creative modern twists. More than just a cafe, we're a place where connections are made, ideas are shared, and every guest feels like part of our family.

From our humble beginnings to becoming your favorite coffee destination - welcome to FIVESIX, where every sip is an experience.
